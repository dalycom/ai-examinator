# Privacy Notice (Patient-facing draft) — AI Examinator

- **Status:** Draft for legal localization (EN master; AR/FR translations required before pilot)
- **Last updated:** 2026-06-21

---

## Who we are

Your healthcare provider uses **AI Examinator** to help clinicians document consultations securely. Your provider is the **data controller**. The platform operator acts as a **processor** under contract.

## What we collect

- Identity and contact details you provide to the clinic
- Clinical information you share during consultations
- Audio recordings **only when you have given consent**
- Transcripts and clinical notes prepared by your care team

## Why we use it

- To deliver safe, accurate medical care and maintain your health record
- To produce draft documentation that **your doctor always reviews**
- AI features produce **suggestions only** — they do not replace your doctor

## AI transparency

Suggestions from AI are clearly labeled. Your doctor must approve, edit, or reject them before they become part of your signed record.

## Your rights

Depending on applicable law (UAE PDPL, GDPR, etc.), you may have rights to:

- Access a copy of your data (export)
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request erasure where legally permitted
- Withdraw consent for recording or AI processing (future processing stops)

Contact your clinic's privacy office to exercise these rights.

## Retention

Records are kept according to clinical and legal retention requirements configured by your organization.

## International transfers

Pilot deployment assumes **UAE-region residency** unless your contract specifies otherwise. See [data-residency-decision.md](./data-residency-decision.md).
